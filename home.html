About the College

Ladakh Polytechnic College is one of the leading technical institutes in Ladakh. It provides diploma courses in different engineering branches. Students can learn practical and technical skills for their future careers.


The college campus is located in a beautiful region of Ladakh.
It has classrooms, laboratories, workshops, and a library.
